Welcome to a Spiritual Journey  
   

Welcome to The Abode - Indian Spiritual tradition has propounded four Purusharth (objectives) of a human life. These being Dharm (Righteousness), Arth (Material Pursuits), Kaam (Contentment) and Moksh (Enlightenment). Bhagwan Nityanand is believed to grant all the four boons to those who visit His Holy Abode.

 

He is considered to fulfill anything and everything that a person wishes for in life, in a righteous way. It is an experience of all, that no one goes empty handed from His Great Pilgrimage.
