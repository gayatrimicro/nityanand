Bhagwan Nityanand the divine

Print Page
The Devine Soul

 

By Divine will the divine took on the body of Nityanand. He was perfect, even though appeared limited in a body. Innumerable people met Nityanand from his early childhood and felt divine attraction and reverence for him. Any incident, any of his talking or his conduct can be truthfully analyzed and we discover the divinity behind everything. Nityanand was an in exhaustible reservoir of love and compassion who had taken on a body to take his devotees towards the divine. He was different to every individual and He guided each individual as per the individual’s capacity and inclination. His subtle presence and stillness gave the inward guidance to the devotee. His few simple words, when uttered, were potent like a hydrogen bomb. Simply watching the devotion of multitudes coming for darshan and Bhagwan's compassion could turn a non-believer to believe.

Devotees become calm in his presence .Peace and joy filled their heart. Their worldly wishes were fulfilled and they turned God-ward. Bhagwan Nityanand's mission was to arouse the divinity in his devotees .Some deep and mystical sayings of Bhagwan have been recorded as a small book titled Chidakash Geeta.
