Darshan

Print Page
A magical place

Indian and foreign devotees travel to the holy temple of Bhagwan Nityanand for Darshan. It is a mysterious practice. It is being in the holy company of the divine. Devotees look at the lustrous murti of Nityanand with reverence and love and feel great peace and stillness. Some have tears of love in their eyes; others sit down and glide into meditation. Some perform pujas and Aartis, others sing and dance in ecstasy. Special darshan, with charan sprash is available to everyone on Gurupurnima and Punyatithi.

Prasad and Souvenirs

Devotees receive blessings in the prasad. Clothes offered to Bhagwan & worn by Bhagwan are made available at a nominal price to deserving & desiring devotees. Ladoo prasad is also available. Both prasads are provided at the enterence to the office (behind the samadhi temple) for the convinience of devotees.

The Chidahash Gita and books on the life of Baba Nityanand are available at the entrance to the sanstha office and in kailash niwas. The vibration (the real prasad) are so powerful that even first time visitors simply sit down here, close their eyes and go in to meditation.

On the day, a devotee arangees Balbhojan; The devotees is given Prasad. If the devotee has not been able to come personally to take prasad. It is sent by post in the normal mail envelope. Similarly efforts are made to send prasad to those sending donations by post.

In the shopping Arcade nearby photos, bhajan/kirtan tapes, Japmalas, murits, silver items like Deepak, agarbati stand and so many other variety of souvenirs are on sale.
