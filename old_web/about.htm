About Sanstha

Print Page
A magical place

Shree Bhimeshwar Sadguru Nityanand Sanstha, in Ganeshpuri is a charitable public trust (P.T.R No 289 Thana), registered under the Bombay Public Trust Act,1950. Light of God shines in Ganeshpuri as Bhagwan Nityanand- A great saint Bhagwan Nityanand took Mahasamadhi in 1961, the devotees in ever-increasing numbers come to Ganeshpuri seeking His blessings.

The Sanstha endeavors to provide helpful conditions for devotees to feel the vibration, purity and peace of the divine presence. The Samadhi Shrine is the heart of Ganeshpuri. Daily Aartis, Pujas, Mantra recitation, Bhajan/Kirtans & Darshan are permeated by Bhagwan's Shakti. Devotees feel the inner stillness even with hundreds present.

Sanstha maintain Samadhi Shrine, Krishna temple and Bhadrakali temple and the natural hot water Kundas where devotees bathe. Facilities like reception, accommodation, meals, public toilet, parking, prasad and dispensary are provided. Balbhojan and Balshikshan so loved by Bhagwan Nityanand are being maintained in the same spirit.

The temple is open from 4.15 a.m. to 9.30 p.m. Bhagwan's darshan is closed for one hour when curtains are drawn across for one hour i.e. from 1.30 p.m. to 2.30 p.m.
