Privacy Policy

Print page

Nityananda.org is committed to the privacy of visitors to  "Bhagawan Nityananda :: Official site for Shree Bhimeshwar Sadguru Nityanand Sanstha".

It is the policy of Nityananda.org not to share your name or information with any unaffiliated individual or organization. If for some reason this policy would change in the future, Nityananda.org will not share any identifiable information about you without your permission.

Registration Forms
There is no registration necessary to visit or participate in this Website. However, certain portions of this site may condition your access on the provision of personally identifiable information. Also, portions of the site allow you to join our mailing list and receive e-mail or regular mail information from us. These same sections provide for the ability to opt-out of receiving future mailings at any time. When making purchases from the bookstore, users will be required to provide contact information such as their name, email, and postal address and financial information such as credit card numbers. Financial information that is collected is used solely to bill the user for products and services.

External Links
This site may contain links to other web sites. Nityananda.org is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such Web sites.


Policies for children (Individuals Under 13 years of Age)

This website does not target children (under age 13) for collection of information online. We do not solicit or knowingly collect user identifiable information on children under the age of 13.  We do not knowingly accept online requests for information from any of our departments from guests under 13 years of age.


Additional Privacy Protections for Children

We encourage children to ask parental permission before sending any information about themselves (name, address, e-mail address, etc.) to anyone over the Internet. We encourage Parents/Guardians to get involved with your children's online usage and to be aware of the activities in which they are participating.
