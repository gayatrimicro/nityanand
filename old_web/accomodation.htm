Accommodation

Accommodation is available to suit all pockets. Accommodation is available for families in Sanstha Buildings named Banglorewala buildings Nityanand Prasad, Nityanand Chayya, Nityanand Jyoti, Nityanand Smirti, Nityanad Krupa as follows:

Banglorewala rooms attached to hall if taken separately whenever desired & available. Extra sleeping mats are provided at nominal cost. 2 Attached bath/toilet 80

b) Large parties can get accommodation as given below :-

Nityanand chayya terrace with 2 Baths and Toilets capacity 50 rent 400/-
Bangalorewala Building Hall with 2 rooms attached/Rooms have bath/toilet attached capacity 40 + 2 rooms rent 400/-  

c) Accommodation allotment is made on first come ,first served basis. Depending on availability advance reservation can be made provided bank draft for charges mentioned above is received favoring: Shri Bhimeshwar Sadguru Nityanand Sanstha

 

For advance booking following procedure is to be followed:

a) Telephone Sanstha reception: +91 02522 261231 / 261523 and confirm desired accommodation is available on required dates.

b) Inform reception  and register your requirement and give your full postal address, telephone no. etc and time of arrival and check amount to remit.

c) Send required amount by bank draft. Firm booking will be made on receipt of bank draft.

 

Registration

Devotees wishing to stay should on arrival, contact the reception desk in the Sanstha office. Reception is open from 6a.m. to 10p.m. They should provide details of any advance payments made.

After the accommodation is selected a Sanstha watchman will escort the devotees to their accommodation. Any queries will be answered in reception. The devotees should familiarize themselves with various places of worship and facilities at the reception.
