Guru Purnima

2005
This full moon day is traditionally dedicated to offering gratitude to the Guru, whose grace bestows spiritual awakening and supports a seeker's effort on the spiritual path. During Gurupurnima, we can contemplate the Guru-disciple relationship and honor all that we have received from the Swami Nityanand.We can resolve to uphold the mission through each means available to us — through dakshina, through seva, and through our dedication to applying the teachings in our lives.
