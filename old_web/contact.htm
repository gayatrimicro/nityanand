Ganeshpuri is a small township about seventy-five kilometers from Mumbai and eighteen kilometers from Bhivandi. It comes under Thane district, that lies in the northwest of Maharashtra state. The modes of transport in Thane district are roads and railways. We find many roads and railway here as Thane district lies close to Mumbai City and Mumbai suburban districts.

Approach for help / Complaints/Suggestion/Queries

Although the Sanstha has provided many facilities for the comfort of the devotees, yet there may occasionally be need for obtaining help, information or assistance. For obtaining any kind of assistance or for offering your valuable suggestions or even for lodging your complaints for quick redressal of your grievances, sanstha can be contacted.

In addition, complaint and suggestion register is available in Sanstha office. Devotee complaints, suggestions or feedback received through registers or via e-mail or ordinary mail are carefully scanned and used for guidance in improving infrastructure and facilities for the devotees.

Lost and Found

It has often been noticed that devotees, sometimes, lose or misplace important items like watches, cameras or other belongings. While the golden rule as well as the advice of the Sanstha would be to be extra cautious regarding important items, particularly at crowded places, yet, in case of such an eventuality the devotees need not panic. The items lost by the devotees, if found by the Sanstha staff or returned to the Sanstha staff by other visiting devotees, are kept in safe custody in the office of the Sanstha. These lost found items are maintained there for some time and, if not claimed, are later put to best use of of the Sanstha. Any claimant, may after such inquiry as satisfies the officer of the sanstha claim his lost and found item as soon as possible after the lost. perishable goods whenever possible are utilized before spoiling.

Evidently, the above course of action is taken only if the items found are not perishable. In case of perishable items, they are deemed to have been exhausted and on claim of such items, the Board may not be in a position to return the same to the devotees.

Cases have also been reported where devotees lose their entire packet of money or valuables and are then left with hardly any amount. Assistance in such cases can be sought from the Sanstha office to contact near and dear ones.

What to do in case of theft?

In case of Pick Pocketing or theft of luggage, devotees may approach the local Police administration.
