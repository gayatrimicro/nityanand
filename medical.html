Medical Facility

Dispensary:

Medical Care and Check Ups

Eye camps are held where the doctors are invited to support the mission. Blood Donation and Dental are organized with the help of Gram Panchayat.


Hospital Project

Very few Medical facilities exist in and around Ganeshpuri. The Trustees have taken the initiative to revive the work for the Hospital Project. The civil work of the hospital commenced on 2011 and will be commissioned soon.

This is a major initiative undertaken by the Trustees for  reviving the 2200 sq. ft hospital.

BHAGWAN NITYANAND

The great guru Swami Nityanand sanctified Ganeshpuri. The place is so beautiful - so tranquil and serene that as soon as you enter the temple you feel the world slipping away and calm & serenity descending on you.

Nitya-Eternal, Ananda-Bliss - Eternal Bliss took a body and was known as Bhagwan Nityanand.
