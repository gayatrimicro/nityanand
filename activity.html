      Children's Park

      The New Children's park was built with a view to provide good entertainment facilities as also to support the ecology by planting trees. There is a small mini train that goes around in park. An impressive gigantic fountain has also been built in the centre of the park. This park enhances serenity to the environment of the Trust.

       

      Bal Bhavan

      The old Annapurna building has been given an extension and will be completed shortly. This building will be dedicated for the children activities of Ganeshpuri and will be known as Nityananda Bal Bhavan.

      New Library & Auditorium

      The old depilated building of the museum was renovated and a grand library has been built with all the hi-tech facilities.  The library was boosted with a surrounding of beautiful gardens and a water fall.  The walls of the building are painted and decorated with the local art the Warli Art, hereby giving students exposure to the local art and their own culture.

BHAGWAN NITYANAND

The great guru Swami Nityanand sanctified Ganeshpuri. The place is so beautiful - so tranquil and serene that as soon as you enter the temple you feel the world slipping away and calm & serenity descending on you.

Nitya-Eternal, Ananda-Bliss - Eternal Bliss took a body and was known as Bhagwan Nityanand.
